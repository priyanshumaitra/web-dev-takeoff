Admission Details

Admission Criteria (1st Year)

Through JEXPO conducted by WBSCT&VE&SD. Criteria: Passed in Madhyamik or equivalent Xth standard examination (with English, Physical
Science/Science & Mathematics) with minimum 35 % marks in aggregate. Appeared candidates are also eligible to apply. Age: Not less than 15
years as on 1st July of the year of examination.

Admission criteria (2nd year) Voclet

Through VOCLET conducted by WBSCT&VE&SD. Criteria: Must have passed or appeared the (10+2) Examination of the WBSCT&VE&SD (erstwhile,
WBSCVET) or having passed 2 years ITI plus Madhyamik or equivalent examination passed. Appeared ITI candidates are not eligible for VOCLET.
ITI trades must be of continuous 2 years duration and a single certificate should mention that. Candidates having passed vocational course from
Councils/Boards other than WBSCT&VE&SD (VED) ,erstwhile, WBSCVE&T are not eligible.Age: No age criteria.
