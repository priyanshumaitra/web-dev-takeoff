Central Calcutta Polytechnic

Central Calcutta Polytechnic is 57 years old with a rich culture and a long tradition. We started with 90 students in 1963 and now it has grown to 225 students per year in January 2020. The college has only a day shift, offering diploma courses in Mechanical, Electrical, Computer Science and Technology as well as
Electronics and Telecommunications.
